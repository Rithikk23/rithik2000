## About Me

Senior AI / ML Engineer with 9+ years of experience building production-grade machine learning, NLP, and Generative AI systems across insurance, healthcare, banking, and e-commerce. I specialize in RAG architectures, multi-agent workflows, LLM evaluation, MLOps, and secure cloud deployment for regulated environments.

I like turning messy enterprise data into systems that are reliable, explainable, observable, and useful in the real world.
